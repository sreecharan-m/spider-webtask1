Welcome To Bubble Trouble
You can find the instructions to play the game here after reading start to play
  • As Soon As the Game Starts You Need to Act Fast And Click As Many Bubbles as Posssible
  • As the Time Progresses More Bubbles Would Be Pushed Into
  • You can Click anywhere inside the Bubble To Make it Disappear,On the Circumference Also
  • You can Pause The Game Using Space Bar And Resume It With The Same
  • The Score is Calculated Based On The Number Of Bubbles You Have Clicked
  • The More Faster You Are The More Bubbles You Get And The More You Score
